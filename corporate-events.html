Why Choose Mahua House

Corporate Retreats in a
Real Forest

Adjoining Reserved Forest

Mahua House sits adjoining Samardha Reserved Forest on Raisen Road, Bhopal — a natural, undistracted environment that boosts creativity, focus, and team bonding.

Meeting Spaces with Full AV

Open and covered meeting areas equipped with projector, sound system, and whiteboards — perfect for strategy sessions, presentations, and workshops away from the office.

Team Activities Built In

Swimming pool, rain dance, jungle trekking, bonfire, badminton, cricket, volleyball, table tennis and more — activities that break barriers and build teams naturally.

Customised Dining (₹1,000/Head)

Traditional home-style food, multi-cuisine buffet, and customised menus for groups — Bed Tea, Breakfast, Lunch, Hi Tea, and Dinner available at ₹1,000 per head.

Stay On-site

Super Deluxe AC, Deluxe AC, and Independent Cottage options from ₹2,500/night (double occupancy). Check-in 11AM, Check-out 10AM.
