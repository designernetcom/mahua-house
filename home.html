Welcome to Mahua House

The Nostalgic Woodland Retreat

Nestled at the edge of Samardha Reserved Forest in Bhopal, Mahua House is a 3.5-acre eco-tourism haven. We believe the finest hospitality is the kind that nature itself provides — warm, honest, and deeply restorative.

Named after the ancient Mahua trees that grace our grounds, our retreat offers forest stays, destination weddings, farm-to-table dining, and unforgettable outdoor experiences — all within reach of the city, yet a world apart.

  • 3.5 acres of pristine woodland in Bhopal district
  • Surrounded by Mahua, Teak and Bamboo groves
  • Eco-conscious design — minimal footprint, maximum experience
  • Independently owned & operated since 2014
