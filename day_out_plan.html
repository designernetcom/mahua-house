Activities

What You Can Do Here

Birdwatching

150+ bird species recorded. Early morning guided sessions with binoculars provided by our resident naturalist.

Nature Trails

Guided walks through the Samardha Reserved Forest. Learn about flora, fauna, medicinal plants and forest ecology.

Forest Pool

A stunning natural-edge pool surrounded by teak trees. Open to day guests between 1 PM and 5 PM with towels provided.

Farm Dining

Breakfast, lunch, and evening snacks prepared with seasonal, locally sourced produce. Dining under the forest canopy.

Campfire Evening

Folk music, chai and stories around the forest campfire. The most memorable part of every visit to Mahua House.
