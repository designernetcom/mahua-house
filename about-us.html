Who We Are

Born from a Love of Forest & Hospitality

Mahua House began as a dream — to create a space where guests could truly disconnect from the city and reconnect with the rhythms of nature. Established in 2014 on the fringes of Samardha Reserved Forest in Bhopal, our 3.5-acre retreat was designed to honour the land, not dominate it.

Named after the ancient Mahua trees that dot our grounds — trees that have fed communities and inspired folklore for centuries — Mahua House is more than a resort. It's a living, breathing forest experience where every corner has a story, every path leads to a discovery, and every sunset feels like it was made just for you.

  • 3.5 acres of pristine woodland in Bhopal district
  • Surrounded by Mahua, Teak, and Bamboo groves
  • Eco-conscious design — minimal footprint, maximum experience
  • Independently owned and operated since 2014

What Drives Us

Our Values & Philosophy

Every decision we make — from how we build our cottages to how we serve your meals — is guided by these principles.

Eco Responsibility

We operate with a minimal ecological footprint — rainwater harvesting, composting, solar lighting, and zero single-use plastics. The forest gives us everything; we owe it our care.

Heartfelt Hospitality

Every guest is welcomed like family. From a warm cup of chai on arrival to a personalised farewell, our team puts genuine warmth into every interaction.

Farm-to-Table Food

Our kitchen draws from local farms and the forest itself — wild herbs, seasonal vegetables, and traditional recipes that tell the story of Madhya Pradesh on every plate.

Community First

We employ locally, source locally, and invest locally. Over 90% of our team comes from surrounding villages. Your stay directly supports livelihoods in our community.

The Property

3.5 Acres of Living Forest

Our property borders the Samardha Reserved Forest — one of Madhya Pradesh's protected green corridors. From your cottage window you might spot a peacock, hear the call of a shikra, or watch fireflies light the tree line at dusk.

Experiences That Stay With You

Forest Treks
