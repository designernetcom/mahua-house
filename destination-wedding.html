About Our Venue

Where Nature Becomes Your Wedding Backdrop

Nestled within 3.5 acres of pristine Bhopal forest, Mahua House is the destination wedding venue you've always imagined but never thought possible. Ancient Mahua and Teak trees frame every photograph. The forest air carries the scent of wildflowers. Stars fill the night sky without a city light in sight.

We work with you from the first call to the last dance — coordinating every detail so you can be fully present for the most important day of your life.
