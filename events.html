Why Host With Us

Every Event is a Meaningful Experience

Adjoining Reserved Forest

Mahua House sits adjoining Samardha Reserved Forest, Raisen Road, Bhopal — MP Tourism accredited, offering a pristine natural backdrop unlike any banquet hall or resort.

Corporate & Group Event Support

Open & covered meeting spaces, projector & sound system, group activities (pool, sports, trekking, bonfire) — everything a team-building retreat or workshop needs.

Authentic Traditional Dining

Customised meal plans at ₹1,000/head — traditional home-style food, multi-cuisine buffet, and special dietary menus. From bed tea & breakfast to hi-tea & dinner.

On-site Accommodation

Guests can stay back in our Super Deluxe AC, Deluxe AC, or Independent Forest Cottages (from ₹2,500/night) — check-in 11AM, check-out 10AM.

Safe, Managed & Family-Friendly

CCTV surveillance, power backup, security staff and responsible management — a trusted, peaceful environment for families, institutions, and corporate groups alike.
