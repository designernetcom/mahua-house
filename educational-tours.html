Why Mahua House

A Classroom Like No Other

Nestled in 3.5 acres adjoining the Samardha Reserved Forest, Mahua House offers a safe, structured and unforgettable outdoor learning environment for students of all ages.

Nature-Based Learning

Hands-on ecology, botany and forest science in a real forest environment — not a classroom.

Jungle Trekking

Group Activities

Team sports, discipline-based programs and collaborative outdoor challenges for student groups.
