Dining Options

Three Ways to Dine With Us

Whether you prefer the comfort of tradition, the variety of a buffet, or a menu tailored precisely to your group — we have you covered.

Traditional Meals

Authentic home-style cooking inspired by Madhya Pradesh's rich culinary heritage — earthy, wholesome and full of flavour. Dal, sabzi, roti, rice and seasonal vegetables, prepared fresh daily.

Multi-Cuisine Buffet

A spread of North Indian, South Indian and continental options — perfect for larger groups, corporate retreats and family stays where everyone has different tastes.

Customised Menus

Planning a corporate event, wedding function or large group visit? We'll work with you to design a menu that fits your occasion, dietary requirements and preferences.

Daily Menu

From Sunrise to Sunset

A full day of authentic flavours — every meal crafted with fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

Early Morning

Bed Tea

Start your forest morning the traditional way — a warm cup of chai served to your room as the forest wakes around you.

  • Fresh masala chai / green tea
  • Light biscuits or dry snack
  • Served at your room / cottage
Morning

Breakfast

A nourishing morning spread to fuel your day of nature adventures, treks and activities.

  • Poha / upma / paratha options
  • Fresh fruit & seasonal items
  • Chai, coffee & fresh juice
  • Continental items on request
Afternoon

Lunch

A hearty traditional thali that brings the authentic flavours of Madhya Pradesh to your plate.

  • Dal, sabzi, roti & rice
  • Seasonal vegetable preparations
  • Salad, raita & pickle
  • Dessert on special occasions
Evening

Hi Tea

Relax after a day of activities with a forest-side evening tea and light snacks as the sun dips below the canopy.

  • Masala chai or herbal teas
  • Samosa, pakora or local snacks
  • Served in the open garden area
Night

Dinner

End your forest day with a memorable dinner — warm, freshly cooked and served under the stars with the sounds of the forest as your backdrop.

  • Full traditional meal spread
  • Rotis, rice, dal & sabzi
  • Special non-veg options on request
  • Bonfire dining experience available
  • Group / buffet format for events
