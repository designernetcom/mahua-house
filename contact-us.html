Directions

How to Reach Us

By Car / Taxi

From Bhopal city take the Raisen Road (NH 146). After 22 km, turn left at Haripura village. Mahua House is 3 km into the forest lane. GPS: search "Mahua House Bhopal".

By Train

Bhopal Junction (BPL) is the nearest railway station, ~28 km away. Pre-arrange a taxi or cab via OLA/Uber. Journey takes approximately 45 minutes from the station.

By Air

Raja Bhoj Airport (BHO) is approximately 35 km from Mahua House. Cabs are available at the airport. We can also arrange airport pickup — just let us know in advance.

Resort Pickup

We offer a complimentary shuttle from Bhopal railway station or airport for bookings of 3 nights or more. Contact us when booking to arrange your pickup time.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in is 11:00 AM and check-out is 10:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out can be arranged based on availability — just let us know in advance and we'll do our best to accommodate you.

Is Mahua House suitable for families with children?

Absolutely. Mahua House is a wonderful destination for families. Children love the nature trails, birdwatching, open lawns, and campfire evenings. We have family rooms available and can arrange child-safe activities on request.

Do you offer vegetarian and vegan food options?

Yes. Our kitchen caters to all dietary preferences including vegetarian, vegan, Jain, and gluten-free. Please inform us of any dietary requirements at the time of booking so we can prepare accordingly.

Is there mobile network and internet access on the property?

Mobile connectivity can be patchy inside the deep forest areas of the property — which many of our guests find to be a welcome digital detox! Wi-Fi is available in the common areas and can be provided in rooms on request.

Can I host a corporate retreat or event at Mahua House?

Yes. We regularly host corporate team offsites, leadership retreats, and small conferences. We can arrange open-air meeting spaces, team-building activities, and customised F&B. Contact us to discuss your requirements.

What is the cancellation policy?

Cancellations made 7 days or more before arrival receive a full refund. Cancellations within 7 days are subject to a 50% retention. No-shows are non-refundable. Special event bookings (weddings, large groups) have separate policies — please confirm at the time of booking.
