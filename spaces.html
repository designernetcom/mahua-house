Where Nature Becomes
Your Event Venue

At Mahua House, each of our four named venues has its own identity — designed for different functions, from grand wedding receptions to intimate ceremonies, cultural events to corporate meetings. Every space carries the living energy of the Samardha Reserved Forest.

Whether you're planning a destination wedding at Mahua Vedika, a mehendi at Chaupal-e-Mahua, a haldi at Mahua Ras Vritta, or a sangeet at the Poolside & Forest Edge — nature is your backdrop and tradition is your guide.
