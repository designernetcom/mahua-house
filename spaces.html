Your Event Venue

At Mahua House, we don't believe in ballrooms with manufactured backdrops. Our spaces are carved from 3.5 acres of live forest — every venue comes with the sound of birds, the texture of teak, and skies wide enough to hold your grandest occasion.

Whether you're planning a 300-guest wedding under a canopy of stars, a focused boardroom away from city noise, or a quiet anniversary dinner in a forest clearing — we have the space, the setting, and the team to make it extraordinary.
